1. Products that decreased their total sales in 2021 with respect to the sales in 2019. The first
part of this application considers only the years 2019 and 2021. It selects the Products with
a total number of products sold in 2021 lower than the total number of products sold in
2019. Store the selected Products in the first output folder (one product per output line).
2. Most sold product(s) for each year. The second part of this application considers all input
data. It selects, for each year, the product(s) associated with the highest total annual sales
for that year. For each product and year, the total annual sales for that product is given by
the sum of the number of products sold in all days of that year. Store the result in the
second output folder, as pairs (year, ID of the most sold product for that year).
